✨ Baked with passion since 2020

Cakes With Love was born in the heart of Amanzimtoti, South Africa, with a simple belief: every celebration deserves a personal, delicious touch. What started as a small home bakery has grown into a beloved spot for custom cakes, cupcakes, traditional sweetmeats, and savoury delights.

We pour love into each creation, using only quality ingredients and elegant designs. Whether it's a wedding, birthday, or corporate gathering — we make every moment memorable.
